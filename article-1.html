What Top Candidates in Lagos Want in 2025

5 min read · Published July 2026

Salary is still important, but candidates are looking for more than compensation. They want clarity on growth, stability, and the kind of culture they will join.

For employers, this means hiring should be a thoughtful process. The strongest candidates will often choose the organisation that communicates clearly and shows evidence of a healthy operating environment.

In practice, this means better job briefs, more transparent expectations, and a more considered interview experience. These details influence both attraction and retention.
