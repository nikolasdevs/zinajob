The Real Cost of a Bad Hire in Nigeria

6 min read · Published July 2026

A bad hire can affect team morale, slow delivery, and create a distraction that lasts far longer than the initial recruiting process.

In many organisations, the hidden cost is not only replacement expense. It can include lost productivity, missed deadlines, and the strain placed on existing team members.

Careful screening, reference checks, and role alignment are essential when the stakes are high. Better hiring decisions protect both performance and culture.
